Objective: To link you with appropriate psychological support services.

  • Professional Counseling

    Connect with a licensed therapist for talk therapy.

    Activity: Schedule an appointment with a clinical psychologist or counselor.
    Referral: Contact local Mental Health Clinic or Hospital Psychiatric Unit.

    Activity: Visit a General Practitioner (GP) for a check-up.
    Focus: Heart rate, sleep patterns, and physical fatigue.
